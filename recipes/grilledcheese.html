Grilled Cheese

If you are wondering how to make a grilled cheese sandwich, Jack is your guy.
He makes one so often that we have a dedicated grilled cheese pan in our kitchen

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Spread mayo on the outside of the bread.
  2. Add grated cheese.
  3. Choose the right skillet.
  4. Go low and slow.
  5. Cover the pan.
  6. Adjust the heat as needed.
